# Abstract and Keywords

## Abstract

Currently, fast orders, proper inventory management, and effective
analytics are necessary for retail and e-commerce business activities.
For many small and midsize enterprises, product data management, sales,
demands, and inventory management operations are still carried out
manually, using spreadsheets or calculations of one kind or another. In
this context, the common issues faced by such businesses are order
confirmation delays, improper inventory visualization, no awareness of
demands, and the inability to make any business decisions timely.

In connection with the above-stated, it is suggested to create an Amazon
Store Business Intelligence and Inventory Management Platform based on
Streamlit software. In this regard, two interfaces will be implemented:
for customers and employees. In particular, the former should have
product viewing, search, classification, selection of quantity, order
confirmation, and inventory updating. On the other hand, the latter will
have the following functionalities: inventory-related dashboarding,
alerts, analytics, products' classification according to ABC rule,
calculations of economic order quantity, KPI monitoring, and reports for
executives. The products’ data should be taken from CSV files, cleansed,
transformed, and analyzed regarding demands and profits. Transactions
will be recorded automatically.

It is important to note that the platform is not a forecasting model but
rather a practical Business Intelligence application. In this regard, it
can be said that it integrates users' actions and data dashboarding for
facilitating decision-making process and helping users understand
internal processes within the organization. It is expected that the
result of this work will be efficient decision-making thanks to
analytics in terms of inventory management.

## Keywords

Business Intelligence; Streamlit; E-Commerce; Inventory Management;
